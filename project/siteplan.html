World Explorer Travel - Website Planning Document

Site Name

World Explorer Travel

Reason: This name reflects the goal of the website—to help visitors explore famous tourist attractions around the world.

Site Purpose

  • Provide information on popular tourist attractions worldwide.
  • Showcase images of landmarks and destinations.
  • Allow visitors to share their travel experiences through a contact form.
  • Help travelers plan trips or learn about new destinations.

Scenarios

  • Which tourist attractions are must-see in Europe?
  • Can I submit my own travel experience or photo to the website?

Color Scheme

  • Blue (#1E90FF) – main headings and navigation links
  • Light Yellow (#FFFACD) – background for content sections
  • Orange (#FFA500) – buttons and accents

Typography

  • Roboto – body text
  • Montserrat – headings

Wireframes

Mobile Layout: Header, hamburger menu, stacked content, image gallery, contact form, footer

Desktop Layout: Top navigation bar, hero image, grid of destination images, sidebar, contact section, footer
